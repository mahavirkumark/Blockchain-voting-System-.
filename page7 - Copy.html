    Privacy Policy for Blockchain Voting
    
    1. **Data Collection**
       - **Personal Information**: We collect necessary personal information for voter verification, such as identity documents and contact details
       - **Voting Data**: Votes cast by users are recorded on the blockchain
    
    2. **Data Usage**
       - **Verification**: Personal information is used to verify voter eligibility
       - **Vote Recording**: Votes are securely recorded and encrypted on the blockchain to ensure privacy and integrity
    
    3. **Data Security**
       - **Encryption**: All personal and voting data is encrypted to prevent unauthorized access
       - **Access Control**: Only authorized personnel can access personal and voting information, with strict controls and monitoring
    
    4. **Data Retention**
       - **Duration**: Personal data is retained only as long as necessary for verification and compliance with legal requirements
       - **Deletion**: Personal data is deleted or anonymized after the retention period or upon request, where feasible
    
    5. **Data Sharing**
       - **Third Parties**: Personal and voting data are not shared with third parties except as required by law or for system maintenance with strict 
       confidentiality agreements
    
    6. **User Rights**
       - **Access**: Users can request access to their personal information
       - **Correction**: Users can request corrections to their data if inaccurate
       - **Deletion**: Users may request deletion of their personal data in accordance with applicable laws
    
    7. **Compliance**
       - **Regulations**: Adheres to relevant data protection regulations and standards
       - **Updates**: Privacy policy is updated as necessary to comply with legal changes and to improve data protection practices
    
    8. **Contact Information**
       - **Support**: Users can contact us with questions or concerns about privacy and data protection
    
    This policy ensures that personal and voting data are handled with the utmost care to protect voter privacy and system integrity.
